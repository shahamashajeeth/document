    SHERLOCK the life of Queen Elizabeth II from her wedding in 1947 to the present day. The first season depicts events up to 1955, with Claire Foy portraying the Queen in the early part of her reign. The second season is intended to cover from the Suez Crisis in 1956 through the retirement of the Queen's third Prime Minister, Harold Macmillan, in 1963 following the Profumo affair political scandal. A third season will include Princess Margaret's five-year affair with baronet and gardening expert Roddy Llewellyn.
Genre: Biopic, Drama
Created by: Peter Morgan
Written by: Peter Morgan
Starring: Claire Foy, Matt Smith
Theme music composer: Hans Zimmer, Rupert Gregson-Williams
Country of origin: United Kingdom, United States
Original language(s): English
